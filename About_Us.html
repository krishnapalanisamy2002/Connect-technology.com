Diversity & Inclusion

We understand the significance of drawing the circle wider to broaden our perspective, to connect on a deeper level, and to be a force for positive change.

For us, diversity, equity and inclusion are more than guiding principles. They are central to our continued growth. We firmly believe that embracing diversity and inclusion (D&I) drives innovation and improves outcomes.

Going further, as a Certified Minority Owned Business Enterprise (MBE) and a Certified Small Business Enterprise (SBE), we engage & utilize Woman Owned Business Enterprise (WBE), Disabled Business Enterprise (DBE), Veteran Owned Business Enterprise (VBE) & Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Business Enterprise (SDVBE) information technology specialists and create a robust environment for all of our consultants to work together & succeed.

Partnerships

Successful development agenda requires inclusive partnerships — at the global, regional, national and local levels — built upon principles and values, and upon a shared vision and shared goals placing people and the client at the center. With this world view, we’ve partnered with industry leaders in multiple industries to bring the best of the solutions & services to our clients.

Community Giving

As a responsible corporate citizen, Connect Technology consistently supports the communities in which we operate through financial assistance, volunteering, community education and green space creation.
